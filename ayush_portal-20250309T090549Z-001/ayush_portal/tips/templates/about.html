About Us

The Ministry of Ayush was formed on the 9th of November 2014 with a vision of reviving the profound knowledge of our ancient systems of medicine and ensuring the optimal development and propagation of the Ayush systems of healthcare. Earlier, the Department of Indian System of Medicine and Homeopathy (ISM&H) formed in 1995, later on, was renamed as the Department of Ayurveda, Yoga, and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy (Ayush) in November 2003. For more details, visit https://ayush.gov.in.
