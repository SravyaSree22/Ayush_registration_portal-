Ayurvedha

Ayurveda is a traditional system of medicine that has been practiced in India for thousands of years. It focuses on balance in the body's systems and emphasizes a holistic approach to health and well-being. Ayurveda utilizes natural remedies such as herbs, diet, and lifestyle changes to maintain and restore health.
