Unani

Unani medicine is a traditional system of medicine based on the teachings of Greek physicians, particularly Hippocrates and Galen, and it has been developed and enriched in India by scholars like Avicenna. The system focuses on the balance of the four humors—blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile—and aims to maintain harmony in the body through the right diet, lifestyle, and natural remedies. It includes a range of practices, from herbal treatments to therapeutic techniques, and it emphasizes preventive care and holistic health. Unani medicine is widely practiced in many parts of India and is recognized under the AYUSH system.
